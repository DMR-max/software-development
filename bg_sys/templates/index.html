About Us
Hi! Welcome to our website! We are Ruzanna Baghdasaryan, Sjouk Ketwaru, Joris Lans, Lisa Pomp and Ryan Sleeuwaegen. We study Data Science & Artificial Intelligence at Leiden University and we made this website for our Software Development course. For this class, we were free to make any program that we wanted, as long as it made use of a dataset. So we decided to make a website that could generate recommendations for board games, since we all enjoy playing those. We hope that this site will help everyone find their perfect board game, so that they can have as much fun as we always do!
About the site
If you want to play a board game with your family friends, or maybe on your own, but you are not sure which one, this site can help you! All you have to do, is press the "get started" button. This will lead you to a couple of questions. The first questions will be some basics about the group that you're playing with. After that you will get some questions to find a game that fits your preferences perfectly. Once you've answered all of the questions, you will get a number of recommendations. So what are you waiting for? Press the button!
