Information

Welcome to the general questions. These will make sure that you only recieve recommendations that fit your specific group of people. If you do not understand the question you can press the ? for more information.

Number of players

Move the slider to indicate the amount of people that you want to play the game with.

Age

Move the slider to indicate the age of the youngest player that is going to play the game.

Duration

Move the sliders to indicate what the minimum and the maximum time is that the game should last.

Difficulty

Move the sliders to indicate what the minimum and the maximum difficulty level of the game should be.

Left: Super easy.
Center: Not too easy, not too difficult.
Right: Extremely difficult.

Watch Out!

If you fill in 300 as a maximum, you will also get games that last for 300+ minutes!
